The Problem

Most protein products aren't built for life after 50.

Gym-focused protein powders are designed for young athletes, not aging adults. They're heavy, artificial, and ignore the specific needs of the Indian body at 50+. Sthira Nutrition is being built differently — from the ground up for this stage of life.

Made for Athletes, Not for Life After 50
